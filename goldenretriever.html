The Golden Retriever

The retriever is a domesticated canine, beloved by many pet owners.
Originally bred for bringing back hunting prizes, they are now one of America's number one pets

Normally the breed has long silky soft golden fur.
However, some dogs have shown to have shorter hair, and even a redder color. The colors and characteristics of a dogs fur also differs through its life span:

  • Young
  • When younger, the hair is shorter, and yellower
  • Middle Age
  • This is when the longer, more golden fur starts to come in, and there is also more fur on the face and nose
  • Old Age
  • Spots of gray start to show on the nose,
